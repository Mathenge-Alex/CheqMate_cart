About CheqMate

CheqMate is an innovative self-checkout system designed to streamline the shopping experience in retail environments. By allowing customers to scan items and checkout directly from their mobile devices, CheqMate enhances convenience, reduces wait times, and improves overall customer satisfaction.

Key Features

  • Mobile Scanning: Customers can scan products using their smartphones for a quicker shopping experience.
  • Real-Time Cart Management: The system updates the user's cart as items are scanned, showing the total cost instantly.
  • Secure Payment Processing: Supports various payment methods for secure transactions via mobile devices.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design guides users through the checkout process.
  • Packaging Verification: Ensures only paid items are packaged, preventing losses.
